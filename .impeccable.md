## Design Context

### Users
Visitors evaluating Adi through writing, recommendations, and crafted projects. Primary job: get a strong feel for taste, technical depth, and personality quickly. Secondary job: browse longer-form thoughts and leave recommendations.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, curious, handcrafted. Should feel thoughtful and slightly playful, but still sharp and technically credible. The Codex metaphor is core: agent workspace, command surface, trace, output, and strong technical signal.

### Aesthetic Direction
Codex-inspired agent workspace with moments of surprise. Dense but readable command chrome, dark technical atmosphere, sharp green/cream accents, and one or two technically memorable interactions. Avoid startup-landing-page tropes, generic SaaS polish, purple gradients, and noisy particle gimmicks.

### Design Principles
1. Reading first. Effects should amplify navigation and authorship, never compete with text.
2. One memorable moment per surface. Concentrated ambition beats scattered cleverness.
3. Physicality over decoration. Motion should feel tied to the agent/workspace metaphor.
4. Progressive enhancement only. Baseline experience must stay fast, clear, and accessible.
5. Taste as product. Every interaction should reinforce editorial judgment and technical craft.
